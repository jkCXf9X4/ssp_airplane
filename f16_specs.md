
# F-16 Fighting Falcon

## General

* Type: Single-engine multirole fighter
* Crew: 1

---

## Dimensions

* Length: 15 m
* Wingspan: 10 m
* Height: 5 m

---

## Weights

* Empty weight: 8,573 kg
* Maximum takeoff weight: 21,772 kg

---

## Performance

* Maximum speed: Mach 2.0+ (~2,120 km/h at altitude)
* Combat radius: 550–800 km (depending on mission type)
* Ferry range: ~3,700 km with external tanks
* Service ceiling: 15,240+ m
* Rate of climb: ~15,240 m/min
* Load factor: +9 g

---

## Powerplant

* Engine: Pratt & Whitney F100-PW-229 or GE F110-GE-129

* Maximum afterburning thrust: ~ **129.7 kN** (≈ 29,160 lbf) ([prattwhitney.com][1])
* Dry (non-afterburning) thrust: Various sources list ~ 17,800 lbf (~ 79 kN) for this family. ([Purdue Engineering][2])
* Engine weight (maximum spec): ~ **1,735 kg** (≈ 3,826 lbf) ([prattwhitney.com][1])
* Engine length: ~ 4.85 m (≈ 191 in) ([prattwhitney.com][1])
* Inlet diameter: ~ 0.88 m (34.8 in) ([prattwhitney.com][1])

Thrust-to-weight ratio for this engine is ~7.6:1. ([prattwhitney.com][1])

---

### Fuel Capacity – Internal & External

* Internal fuel capacity (for many F-16Cs): ~ **3,160 kg** (≈ 6,970 lb) ([Aerospaceweb][3])
* Some sources list internal fuel ~ **3,249 kg** for F-16C. ([Scribd][4])
* External fuel capacity (drop tanks) in addition: ~ **3,066 kg** (≈ 6,760 lb) per some sources. ([sirviper.com][5])
* Typical internal + two external tanks figure: ~ **5,443 kg** (≈ 12,000 lb) internal + external. ([Air Force][6])

---

## Avionics

* Fly-by-wire: Full digital system

---

## Armament

* Hardpoints: 9 total
* Max external payload: ~7,700 kg
